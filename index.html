Do you like to travel, would like to share your adventures with the world and could use some extra cash? E-Tour-Guide.com is the place for you! If own Google Glass or another device capable of recording video, we will pay you to take it on holidays with you and share parts of your journey with the world!

Or perhaps you would like to travel, but don't have the time or money? E-Tour-Guide.com offers you the opportunity to take part in tours from the comfort of your own home. See the pyramids! Visit the bustling streets of New York! Or be guided around the streets of Paris by our vibrant and enthusiastic tour guides.

How it Works

  1. Our virtual tour operators can create a tour and allow people to join.
  2. Our virtual tourists can discover tours that are interesting to them and sign up.
  3. If the tour is streamed live, the tourists will be notified and connect to the feed when the tour begins.
  4. The tour guide takes their participants on holidays with them, capturing video, audio and pictures.
  5. Before the tour, or during a live tour, participants will have the opportunity to ask questions and provide input and direction the tour.
  6. After the tour, the participants can leave feedback and further questions.
  7. There is a monthly membership fee for both tourists and tour operators who wish to provide or participate in live tours.
  8. Tour operators are paid based on the number of tourists and popularity of their tours.
